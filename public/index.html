Our Story

Is it ice cream, or is it a ministry?

As the Royals like to say — both.

Boogi Bliss began as a love letter from Booboo & GiGi: a way to bring people together over something undeniably joyful, and a way for GiGi to share the good love of Christ — one scoop at a time.

Every cart we wheel up to a wedding or backyard gathering carries the same hope: that the people gathered around it would feel seen, served, and a little more connected than when they arrived.

What We Bring

An experience, not a dessert table.

The Cart

Our signature scallop-trim cart, hand-finished and ready to roll up to your venue. Two scoopers, three flavors at a time, endless smiles.

The Stand

A larger setup for parties of 100+. Up to six flavors, custom signage, and the option to add affogato, milkshake, or sundae bar service.

White-Glove Service

Setup, service, and full cleanup. Compostable cups and spoons. Custom flavor pairings curated to the season and the celebration.

Custom Flavors

Couples and hosts can collaborate with us on a one-of-a-kind flavor named for the day — your bliss, in a waffle cone.
